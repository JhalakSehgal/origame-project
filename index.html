Facts about Camel

  1. Camel has furry ears
  2. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat
  3. Their eyes have three eyelids and two rows of eyelashes that prevent sand to enter their eyes

Facts about Chameleon

  1. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches
  2. Their hands and feet have large toes that help them cling to branches
  3. Chameleons have extremely powerful tongues

Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals
  2. Pigeons are highly sociable animals
  3. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time

Facts about Panda

  1. They have great camouflage for their environment
  2. They spend a lot of their day eating
  3. Bamboo is critical to their diet

Facts about teddy

  1. Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  2. The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  3. On February 15, 1903, toy store owner and inventor Morris Michtom places two stuffed bears in his shop window, advertising them as Teddy bears.

Facts about Cicida

  • Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans
  • Cicadas inundate forests as a survival mechanism
  • Cicadas can't eat or bite because they lack jaws
